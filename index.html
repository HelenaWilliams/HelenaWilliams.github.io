iD Tech

    As a programming instructor for iD Tech, I teach subjects such as Python, Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Roblox/Lua, and Cybersecurity. Every lesson, I'm faced with the unique challenge of communicating difficult concepts to students from all ages and backgrounds. It's this diversity of experiences that makes this job both quite difficult and quite rewarding: some of the curricula I've crafted, in the form of programming projects, is in the "Projects" tab of this website. I've taught over 100 lessons to date, but it never gets old, and the skills I have gained from this job continue to help me outside the office.
